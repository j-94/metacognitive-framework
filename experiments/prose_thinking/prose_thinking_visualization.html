üîç Latent Space Features

How We Measure "Thinking Quality" in Latent Space

  • Abstraction Level: Frequency of abstract concepts (pattern, constraint, recursive)
  • Concreteness: Use of specific values and operations
  • Metaphor Usage: Visual and analogical thinking indicators
  • Logical Operators: Structured reasoning markers
  • Complexity: Thought elaboration and detail

These features combine with test success rates to create a unified "thinking quality" metric!
